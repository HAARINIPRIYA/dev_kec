About the Program

Discover the partnership between Kongu Engineering College and GUVI

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between academic excellence and industry demands by providing world-class DevOps education through a collaborative partnership between Kongu Engineering College and GUVI, empowering students to become skilled DevOps professionals ready for the modern tech industry.

Kongu Engineering College

Kongu Engineering College, established in 1985, has been a beacon of technical education in South India. Located in Erode, Tamil Nadu, the college has consistently delivered quality engineering education with a focus on practical learning and innovation.

With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and industry partnerships, KEC has positioned itself as a premier institution for engineering excellence. The college is committed to developing engineers who are not just technically sound but also ethically responsible and socially conscious.

The DevOps program is a testament to the college's commitment to staying at the forefront of technological advancement and industry trends.

GUVI - VIT

GUVI is the online learning platform of VIT, India's premier university. With a mission to democratize quality education, GUVI has become a leader in online skill development and career transformation.

With over 2 million learners and a focus on industry-relevant skills, GUVI brings cutting-edge curriculum design, experienced mentors, and employment opportunities to students across the globe.

The DevOps program with Kongu Engineering College represents GUVI's commitment to partnering with leading academic institutions to provide the best possible learning experience.
